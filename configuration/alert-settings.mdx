Configure when and how Praydo notifies you about upcoming prayer times. This includes notification timing and adhan (call to prayer) settings.

## Notification Timing

Set how many minutes before each prayer time you want to receive a notification reminder.

<ParamField path="minutes" type="number" default="5">
  Minutes before prayer time to show notification.
  
  **Available Options:**
  - 5 minutes
  - 10 minutes
  - 15 minutes
  - 20 minutes
  - 25 minutes
  - 30 minutes
  
  This setting applies to all enabled prayer notifications.
</ParamField>

## How Notifications Work

Praydo provides two types of alerts:

1. **Pre-prayer Notification**: A reminder shown X minutes before the prayer time (configurable)
2. **Adhan at Prayer Time**: The call to prayer played exactly at the prayer time (if enabled)

### Example Timeline

If Maghrib is at 6:15 PM and notifications are set to 10 minutes:

- **6:05 PM**: Notification appears - "Maghrib prayer in 10 minutes"
- **6:15 PM**: Adhan plays (if enabled for Maghrib in [Prayer Settings](/configuration/prayer-settings))

## Configuring Alert Settings

To configure notification timing:

1. Open **Settings** from the main menu
2. Navigate to the **Notifications** tab
3. Select your preferred notification time from the dropdown:
   - 5 Minutes
   - 10 Minutes
   - 15 Minutes
   - 20 Minutes
   - 25 Minutes
   - 30 Minutes
4. Changes are saved automatically

<Note>
  The notification timing setting applies globally to all prayers that have adhan enabled. Individual per-prayer timing is not currently supported.
</Note>

## Enabling/Disabling Adhan

To control which prayers trigger the adhan:

1. Open **Settings** from the main menu
2. Navigate to the **Prayer Times** tab
3. Under **Play Adhan At**, check the prayers where you want adhan to play:
   - Fajr
   - Dhuhr
   - Asr
   - Maghrib
   - Isha

<Info>
  You must enable adhan for at least one prayer in the Prayer Times settings for notifications to appear.
</Info>

## Common Configuration Scenarios

    - Notification time: 15 minutes

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Long Notice for Work Days">

    - Notification time: 30 minutes

## System Notifications

Praydo uses your system's notification system to display alerts:

- **Windows**: Toast notifications in the system tray
- **macOS**: Notification Center alerts
- **Linux**: Desktop notifications via DBus

<Warning>
  Ensure that Praydo has notification permissions enabled in your system settings, otherwise you may not receive prayer time alerts.
</Warning>

## Notification Permissions

To ensure you receive notifications:

### Windows
1. Open **Settings** > **System** > **Notifications**
2. Find **Praydo** in the app list
3. Enable notifications

### macOS
1. Open **System Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Find **Praydo** in the app list
3. Set notification style to "Alerts" or "Banners"
4. Enable sound if desired

### Linux
1. Notification permissions vary by desktop environment
2. Most Linux distributions allow notifications by default
3. Check your desktop environment's notification settings if alerts aren't appearing

## Autostart Integration

For notifications to work when your computer starts, enable autostart in the [General Settings](/configuration/general-settings):

1. Open **Settings** > **General** tab
2. Enable **Autostart at system startup**
3. Praydo will launch automatically and notifications will work from system startup

<Tip>
  Combine autostart with notification settings to ensure you never miss a prayer time, even right after booting your computer.
</Tip>

## Data Storage

Alert settings are stored locally using Tauri's store plugin:

- **Storage Key**: `alert` and `timeRemaining`
- **Save Strategy**: Debounced (saved 1 second after changes)
- **Persistence**: Settings persist across app restarts

<Note>
  All alert settings are saved automatically. You don't need to click a "Save" button - changes take effect immediately and are persisted in the background.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Notifications Not Appearing">
    **Possible causes:**
    1. System notifications are disabled for Praydo
    2. No prayers have adhan enabled in Prayer Times settings
    3. Focus Assist (Windows) or Do Not Disturb (macOS) is active
    
    **Solutions:**
    1. Check system notification permissions
    2. Verify at least one prayer has adhan enabled
    3. Adjust Do Not Disturb settings to allow Praydo notifications
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Adhan Not Playing">
    **Possible causes:**
    1. No prayers selected in "Play Adhan At" settings
    2. System volume is muted
    3. Audio permissions not granted to Praydo
    
    **Solutions:**
    1. Enable adhan for desired prayers in Prayer Times settings
    2. Check system volume settings
    3. Verify audio permissions in system settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong Notification Timing">
    **Possible causes:**
    1. Notification timing not saved properly
    2. System clock is incorrect
    3. Location or calculation method is wrong
    
    **Solutions:**
    1. Re-select notification timing and wait for auto-save (1 second)
    2. Verify system clock is accurate
    3. Check location and calculation method settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
