## What is Praydo?

Praydo is a simple, modern, and cross-platform desktop application designed to help Muslims stay on top of their daily prayer times. Built with modern technologies like Tauri, SvelteKit, and Rust, Praydo provides an elegant and efficient solution for tracking prayer schedules right from your desktop.

Whether you're at home or traveling, Praydo ensures you never miss a prayer by providing accurate, location-based prayer times with desktop notifications.

## Why Use Praydo?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Simple and Modern Design">
    Praydo features a clean, intuitive interface that makes checking prayer times effortless. No clutter, just the information you need.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cross-Platform Support">
    Built with Tauri, Praydo runs natively on Windows, macOS, and Linux, providing a consistent experience across all platforms.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Accurate Prayer Times">
    Powered by the PrayTimes.org library, Praydo uses proven algorithms to calculate precise prayer times based on your exact location.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lightweight and Fast">
    Thanks to Rust and Tauri, Praydo is incredibly lightweight and fast, using minimal system resources while delivering maximum performance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Privacy First">
    Your location and preferences are stored locally on your device. Praydo respects your privacy and doesn't collect or share your data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
